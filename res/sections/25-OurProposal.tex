To bind physical people to wallet addresses we exploit the fact that
people frequently advertise their own public keys to receive founds
through  donations.

We build our data set by searching onto Twitter and in developers 
repositories.


% TWITTER

for disparate purposes and sometimes the owners of some twitter accounts
decide to donate some altcoins to gain popularity (e.g., with hashtag
\texttt{\#BTCGiveAway}). They advertise 
their intent with a tweet, in which they ask the readers to follow
them and reply with their own altcoin address. After some time the
owner draw from the reply one account and perform the donation.
